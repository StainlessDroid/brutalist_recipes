Mac and cheese


A classic american recipe for hungover sundays

Ingredients

  • Macaroni:
    • On a pinch, almost any pasta will do, but the recipe calls for macaroni or little elbows.
  • Flour and Butter:
    • You'll need butter and flour to make a roux for the cheese sauce.
  • Milk:
    • Use hole milk to get a better flavour and texture.
  • Cheese:
    • This baked mac and cheese calls for cheddar and parmesan, but you can make do with what you have.
  • Bread crumbs:
    • They will make for a perfect crunchy topping to bake
  • Seasonings:
    • Salt and pepper goes into the sauce, while paprika can be sprinkled over the topping.

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain, and transfer to a oven safe baking dish.
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, and stir.
  3. make the bread crumbs topping, spread it over the macaroni and sprinkle with the seasoning of your choice.
  4. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown.
  5. Enjoy!
